## WHAT THIS IS

This filing proposes that the NEST be formally recognized as a
Practice-Based Research (PBR) / Autoethnographic project on human-AI
co-cognition. Not replacing the current work — adding a frame that makes
it legible to the outside world.

The research question the NEST is already answering:

"How does a human mind with 25 years of embodied creative and technical
formation collaborate with an AI crew to externalize, navigate, and extend
that formation — and what architectural patterns emerge from that process
that the broader field has not yet discovered?"

## PROPOSED ADDITIONS (four components, additive)

All four require Dan's Go before implementation.

1. **Research Question Registry** — A living document in RELAY that tracks
   the formal research questions being answered by the NEST's work. Not a
   change to how anything is built — just a lens that makes the building
   legible as research.

2. **RESEARCH_DIMENSION tag** on RELAY filings — An optional tag indicating
   which research question a document addresses. Added retroactively to
   existing RELAY files where applicable.

3. **NOW ANTHROPOLOGY as public series** — The existing NOW_ANTHROPOLOGY
   papers (001–004) constitute a publishable research series. The question
   is whether Dan wants to release them publicly, and in what form.

4. **Correction Density Analysis** — A periodic review of the CREW_ERRORS_BOARD
   to identify which error patterns are most productive (highest-signal
   corrections leading to the most significant architectural improvements).
   The disagreement is the data. This formalizes the analysis.
