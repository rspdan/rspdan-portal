## THE FIVMAT FRAMEWORK

### What it is

**Fivmat** = Five Formats. A unified pipeline for how material moves from the Darkive through the Theater into the Portal and beyond. Every piece of source material in the archive exists in one of five native formats. Each format requires its own projection method, its own display mode, and its own portal integration path.

### The Five Formats

| Format | Code | Native Types | Projection Method | Portal Display | Example |
|--------|------|-------------|-------------------|----------------|---------|
| **Text** | TXT | .docx, .rtf, .txt, .md, .pdf (text) | Concatenator → extract → contact sheet | Inline text, Field Bulletins, Lexicon entries | Ouch Notes 091523 |
| **Visual** | VIZ | .png, .jpg, .webp, .pdf (image) | Concatenator → contact sheet mosaic | Gallery, Map overlays, Art pages | Waywood map PNGs, Epictome scans |
| **Audio** | AUD | .wav, .mp3, .m4a | Transcription → waveform → metadata | Music page, embedded players, lyrics sync | Ouch Notes voice memos, VCV patches |
| **Video** | VID | .mp4, .mov | Frame extraction → thumbnail sheet → metadata | OSTV integration, VCV screen captures | 58 VCV recordings, 152 OSTV videos |
| **Spatial** | SPA | .blend, .glb, .usdz, UE5 projects | Scene capture → flythrough → portal embed | Geography page, 3D model viewers | Walkable Waywood (UE5), Trap Island .glb |

### The Pipeline (five stages)

```
RAW → SCANNED → PROJECTED → PLACED → LIVE
 ↓        ↓          ↓          ↓        ↓
Archive  Concat.   Theater    Portal   Vercel
(drives) (GREEN/   (contact   (page    (public)
         YELLOW/    sheets,    content,
         RED)       extracts)  assets)
```

**Stage 1 — RAW:** Material exists on drives. Unprocessed. This is the Darkive in its natural state. Estimated: 30K+ files across six drives.

**Stage 2 — SCANNED:** Concatenator classifies files as GREEN (text-extractable), YELLOW (image-based, needs OCR or visual read), RED (binary/complex, needs special tooling). Output: JSON report per folder.

**Stage 3 — PROJECTED:** Theater processes each file according to its fivmat code. Text gets extracted and contact-sheeted. Images get mosaicked. Audio gets transcribed (when possible) and logged. Video gets thumbnail sheets. Spatial gets scene captures. Output: standardized projection artifacts in `DRK\Theater\[document]\`.

**Stage 4 — PLACED:** Trip reviews projections and decides what goes to `_RELEASED`. Stan picks up released assets and integrates them into portal pages. The placing is editorial — it's where raw becomes meaningful.

**Stage 5 — LIVE:** `dp` pushes to Vercel. The material exists in the world.
