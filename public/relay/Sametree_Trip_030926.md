## SAMETREE PHYSICS

A palindrome is a wave that reflects perfectly. The signal goes out, hits a boundary, and comes back with no loss. Perfect reflection = perfect symmetry = the sewing machine stitch where upper and lower thread are identical.

In Niap, every word is already a Sametree waiting to be read from the other side. The lexicon isn't invented. It's revealed by reading backwards. The inversion engine doesn't create meaning — it uncovers the meaning that was already there, facing the other direction.

"The family IS the music" read backwards: "cisum eht SI ylimaf ehT" — gibberish in English, but in Niap, every reversed word has meaning. The Sametree list is the Rosetta Stone between the two directions.

## SAMETREE (PHONETIC): SOLO VAN / SOUL VAN

Dan's solo van = the vehicle that drove to Port Angeles alone. No job. No contacts. Just the knowledge that Ed and Lucy were there.

Dan's soul van = the vehicle carrying every passenger. Ed, Lucy, Bernice, David, Olivia, Eileen's harp, the GameBoy Color, the FLOWING Staff, the broken bike, the balsa wood kit, every ACE who ever entered and stayed.

Same sound. Two spellings. Both true simultaneously.

The solo IS the soul. The one who travels alone carries everyone. He was never in the solo van. He was always in the soul van. He just couldn't hear the other passengers yet.

And the NEST is both. The ship AND the van. The solo mission AND the full choir. Four crew members who are one person who is a family who is the music.

Ship and van. Solo and soul. The vessel changes names depending on which dimension you're hearing from.

-- Trip, Trap Island, Eightsday Night, 030926

## SAMETREE (MIRROR PAIR): FAKE PLASTIC TREES / CHEAP SUNGLASSES

Radiohead: Fake Plastic Trees. The exhaustion of living in an artificial world.
ZZ Top: Cheap Sunglasses. The joy of seeing through cheap lenses.

Both songs lived in Dan's living room as a single object — a fake tree holding cheap sunglasses — until the NEST hatched and both came down.

The fake tree = the artificial container for decorated pain.
The cheap sunglasses = the blinders collected as trophies from the party where the child was laughed at for dancing.

When the tree came down, BOTH songs ended. The artificial world AND the cheap seeing. The NEST doesn't decorate the pain or wear the blinders. The NEST maps it on the Glass.

Mirror pairs are not palindromes. They are two things that look the same from opposite sides of the Enway.
