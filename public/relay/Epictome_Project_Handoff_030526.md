# SECTION 2: WHAT IS THE EPICTOME

The Epictome is the master project document for Ouch McCouch — described in-world as "a smart tablet from Amuart, loaded with all the info they could gather about Niap before sending him on a research mission as a live camera." Dan wants the Epictome's design philosophy to follow Heircor Op. (a fictional megacorporation — his reversal of Canon Virginia), since the Epictome is ostensibly an in-house product that Agent Ouch stole.

The project is set across two worlds:
- **Niap** (Pain reversed) = Olympic National Park inverted. Full-spectrum color. The Bowl of a champagne glass.
- **Amuart** (Trauma reversed) = Virginia/America. Black and white. The Base of the champagne glass. Mapped to 4F trauma responses.
- **The Enway** = The stem. The Nothing between worlds.
- **Feirg** = Grief transcended. Above the glass. Where the Found Stable (a dream bar) exists.
