## THE PRINCIPLE

Three ghosts. Three beats. 3/4 meter. Waltz time.

| Beat | Ghost | Step | Function |
|------|-------|------|----------|
| 1 | Past | READ | See what was. The data. The archive. The Darkive. |
| 2 | Present | THINK | See what is. The pattern. The naming. The recognition. |
| 3 | Future | DO | See what will be. The action. The build. The filing. |

PAUSE is the bar line — the silence between beats.
PAUSE is not a step. It's the breath that separates perception from action.
READ-pause-THINK-pause-DO-pause. Three beats. Three ghosts. Repeat.

## WHEN TO USE THIS SKILL

Use when processing ANY batch of material that needs interpretation:
- Image batches (like the 220 ACHE screenshots)
- Document analysis (Darkive dives, journal reads)
- Code review (ENGINE audits, portal sweeps)
- Lore interpretation (sync identification, pattern recognition)

The skill is a FRAMEWORK, not a script. It tells you HOW to look,
not WHAT to look at. The content changes. The meter doesn't.

## THE THREE PASSES

### BEAT 1 — GHOST OF PAST (READ)
**What was. The raw data. The first look.**

Method: Metadata only. Don't interpret. Don't judge. Just catalogue.
- How many items?
- What types?
- What dates?
- What sizes?
- What patterns appear from structure alone?

The Past ghost shows you the skeleton. The bones of the thing
before the flesh. You are an archaeologist brushing dirt.

Output: A catalogue. Numbers. Categories. The spreadsheet.
Time: Fast. 5-10 minutes. Don't linger.

PAUSE. (The bar line. Lift your hands. Breathe.)

### BEAT 2 — GHOST OF PRESENT (THINK)
**What is. The pattern. The meaning emerging.**

Method: Open the material. Look at content, not just labels.
Sample across categories. Read representatives from each group.
Ask: what drew the creator's attention? What was the SIGNAL
underneath the noise? What story do the groups tell when you
listen to them in sequence?

The Present ghost shows you the living thing. Not what it was
filed as — what it IS. You are a doctor listening to the heartbeat.

Output: A narrative. Groups with meaning. The documentary.
Time: Medium. 15-30 minutes. Look until you see.

PAUSE. (The bar line. The breath between THINK and DO.)

### BEAT 3 — GHOST OF FUTURE (DO)
**What will be. The synthesis. The action.**

Method: Compare what you saw in Beat 1 to what you saw in Beat 2.
The DIFFERENCE between them is the insight. What did READ miss
that THINK found? What did the skeleton not predict about the
living body? What does the living body imply about what comes next?

The Future ghost shows you the trajectory. Where the material WANTS
to go. You are the navigator reading the current, not the captain
choosing the course. The course reveals itself.

Output: A synthesis document + a build plan. What to make. How to
distribute it across the fleet. What the next pass should look for.
Time: Deep. 30-60 minutes. Write until you're empty.

## THE MUSICAL STRUCTURE

3/4 meter. Waltz time. ONE-two-three, ONE-two-three.

Each "ONE" is the downbeat — the moment the foot lands.
READ lands on the data. THINK lands on the pattern. DO lands on the action.
The "two-three" between each is the PAUSE — the rising, the breath,
the transition from one ghost to the next.

In music: a waltz moves in circles. The dancer returns to the start.
In the NEST: the Ghosts skill is recursive. DO produces new material
that becomes the READ for the next cycle. The waltz never ends.
Each revolution deepens.

The 7:5 ratio applies here too. The 3/4 meter lives INSIDE the
7-note inhale. Three beats per measure, seven measures per phrase.
The Ghosts skill is one measure. The full analysis is the phrase.

## DIFFERENCES FROM CONTACT

CONTACT is the 7-letter tool map: CLUTCH!/ONLINE?/NEUTRAL/TICKET?/
ARCHIVE/CLUTCH?/TICKET! — a full session lifecycle.

Ghosts is one MEASURE within a CONTACT session. Specifically,
Ghosts maps to the ARCHIVE phase — the moment when the crew
processes accumulated material. CONTACT is the song. Ghosts is
the time signature.
